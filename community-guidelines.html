Community Guidelines

Ensuring a safe, authentic, and secure iReshp community.

At iReshp, our mission is to provide a secure and high-performance social experience[cite: 10]. To maintain the integrity of our chronological feeds and encrypted chats, we expect all users to follow these guidelines[cite: 30, 35].

1. Authentic Identity

iReshp is built on real connections. To keep our community safe:

  • No Impersonation: Do not create accounts to deceive others or pretend to be someone you are not[cite: 14].
  • Accurate Profiles: While bio and profile pictures are optional, any information provided should be authentic and respectful[cite: 15, 16].

2. Respectful Public Content

As iReshp functions as a microblogging platform, public posts in Feeds and Reels are visible to the community[cite: 17, 35]. We strictly prohibit:

  • Hate Speech & Harassment: Content that promotes violence, incites hatred, or targets individuals based on identity.
  • Explicit Content: Posting sexually explicit material or graphic violence.
  • Intellectual Property Theft: Ensure you have the rights to the images, videos, and text you post[cite: 20, 21].

3. Privacy and Security

Our Commitment: We use zero-knowledge architecture to protect your private messages[cite: 29]. We expect you to respect the privacy of others in return.
  • No Doxxing: Do not post the private information of others without their explicit consent.
  • Secure Communication: Do not use iReshp to distribute spam, malware, or phishing links.

4. Enforcement and Reporting

We take violations seriously. Depending on the severity, we may:

  • Remove public content that violates these rules[cite: 57].
  • Suspend or permanently delete accounts found in breach of our Terms of Service[cite: 55].

Users can report content directly in the app or by contacting our team at support@ireshp.com[cite: 61].

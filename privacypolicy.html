iReshp Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Welcome to iReshp. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our mobile application (iReshp), website (ireshp.com), and related services.

By using iReshp, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please discontinue use of the service immediately.

Zero-Knowledge Encryption (Reshlock): Your private communications are protected by End-to-End Encryption. iReshp does not possess the decryption keys required to read your messages. We cannot provide message content to third parties or law enforcement because we do not have access to it.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Provided by You

  • Account Information: We collect your email address, username, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Profile Metadata: You may choose to provide a profile picture, bio, and display name.
  • User-Generated Content: We store posts, reels, and comments you share publicly on the platform.

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Metadata: We collect device IDs, OS versions, and hardware models to prevent fraud (e.g., Honeypot security) and ensure app stability.
  • Usage Logs: We track interactions with the app to improve user experience, such as feature engagement and crash reports.
  • IP Addresses: Collected for security logging and to prevent unauthorized access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow you to sign in and secure your account.
  • Content Delivery: To host and display your Reels and Feed posts to other users.
  • Security & Safety: To detect and block malicious actors, bots, and users violating our Terms of Service via automated security logic.
  • Communication: To send account-related notifications or respond to support inquiries.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide a high-quality service, we share limited data with the following sub-processors:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication, real-time database management, and cloud functions.
  • Cloudinary: Used for the secure hosting and optimization of user-uploaded images and videos (Reels).
  • Cloud Infrastructure: We use secure cloud providers (e.g., DigitalOcean or AWS) for general backend hosting.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera/Microphone: To record Reels and capture profile photos.
  • Photo Library: To upload existing media for posts.
  • Notifications: To alert you of new messages or interactions.

5. Data Retention

  • Public Content: Stored as long as your account is active or until you delete the content.
  • Encrypted Messages: Once delivered to the recipient's device, message content is removed from our servers.
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, all personal identifiers are purged from our production databases within 30 days.

6. Account Deletion & Data Control

In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, we provide clear methods to delete your account and associated data:

In-App Deletion

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will immediately trigger the removal of your profile and content.

Web-Based Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by visiting:

ireshp.com/delete-account

Data Purged

  • Personal info (Email, Username).
  • All public posts and Reels.
  • Follower/Following relationships.

7. Children's Privacy

iReshp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Legal Protections

We reserve the right to disclose your information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our platform. However, as noted, End-to-End encrypted message content cannot be disclosed as we do not have access to it.

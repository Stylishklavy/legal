Privacy Policy for iReshp

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

iReshp is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within our social media and messaging platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your username and authentication details.
  • Messages: We use end-to-end encryption. This means we cannot read the content of your private messages; they are decrypted only on your device and the recipient's device.
  • Local Storage: iReshp uses a local-first architecture (SQLite). Much of your data, including chat history, is stored directly on your device.

2. How We Use Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the iReshp messaging service.
  • Ensure the security of our platform.
  • Deliver a chronological and localized feed experience.

3. Data Security

We prioritize security through end-to-end encryption and secure communication protocols (WebSockets). While we take every precaution, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage users to protect their account credentials.

4. Third-Party Services

iReshp does not sell your personal data to third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (like server hosting) to maintain the service, but they do not have access to your encrypted messages.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the app settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official iReshp support channels.

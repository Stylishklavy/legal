Privacy Policy for iReshp

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

At iReshp, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data across our mobile application and web services.

Note on Encryption: iReshp utilizes end-to-end encryption for private messaging. This means the content of your messages is only readable by you and the recipient. We do not have the technical means to access your private conversations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your username, phone number or email address, and profile metadata provided during registration.
  • Content Storage: While iReshp uses a local-first architecture (storing data on your device via SQLite), certain metadata may be processed by our servers to facilitate message routing and synchronization.
  • Device Information: We may collect info about your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to optimize app performance.
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information ("log data") created by your use of the services to ensure security and prevent platform abuse.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate real-time communication via WebSockets.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To provide a chronological feed of content relevant to your local area or social circle.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because iReshp prioritizes local storage, much of your data remains on your physical device. For data stored on our servers:

  • We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service.
  • Users can delete their account at any time. Upon account deletion, we remove your identifiable data from our active databases within 30 days.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared under the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you choose to share content with other users.
  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party hosting (e.g., DigitalOcean) to maintain our infrastructure. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Security of Data

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit and hashing for stored passwords. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

iReshp is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact the iReshp team through the official support channels in the app or via our website.

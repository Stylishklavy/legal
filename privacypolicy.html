Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Welcome to iReshp. Your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines our commitment to data protection and how we handle information within our localized social and messaging ecosystem.

Encryption & Local-First: iReshp uses end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for chats and a local-first architecture (SQLite). Your private messages are decrypted only on your device and are not accessible by us.

1. Information Collection

To provide a seamless experience, we collect:

  • Account Details: Information provided during signup (username, contact info).
  • Device Metadata: Basic hardware and OS info to ensure app stability.
  • Interaction Data: Non-private metadata required to route messages and update chronological feeds.

2. Use of Data

We utilize your data to:

  • Maintain secure, low-latency communication via WebSockets.
  • Verify account authenticity and prevent spam.
  • Optimize the "local-first" synchronization between your device and our servers.

3. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your information:

  • Access & Export: You can view your data within the app settings.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account at any time. Server-side identifiable data is purged within 30 days of deletion.
  • Local Control: Since chat history is stored locally, you can clear your local database directly from the app.

4. Security Standards

We use industry-standard protocols (SSL/TLS) for data in transit. While no system is perfect, our use of E2EE ensures that the most sensitive part of your data—your conversations—remains private even in the event of a server breach.

5. Third-Party Policy

We do not sell your data. We only use trusted infrastructure providers (like DigitalOcean) to host our backend services. These providers do not have access to your personal content.

6. Contact Support

For any privacy-related inquiries, please reach out through the official iReshp support channels or the contact section on our web portal.

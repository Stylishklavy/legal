iReshp Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

1. Header & Scope

Welcome to iReshp. This Privacy Policy is designed to provide full transparency regarding how iReshp collects, uses, and protects your information[cite: 4]. This policy applies strictly to the iReshp mobile application, the ireshp.com website, and all integrated messaging, social feed, and creator services provided by our platform[cite: 5].

By using iReshp, you agree to the practices described in this document[cite: 6]. We are committed to a "privacy-first" architecture, ensuring that your social interactions are secure and your personal data remains under your control[cite: 7].

The Encryption Pillar: iReshp is built with a zero-knowledge architecture. All one-on-one chats are protected by End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)[cite: 28, 30]. We do not hold decryption keys and cannot access your private messages under any circumstances[cite: 32].

2. Information Collection (The Data Hierarchy)

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Credentials: Phone number and display name used for authentication and spam prevention[cite: 13, 14].
  • User Profile: Optional profile picture and bio, which are public within the ecosystem[cite: 15, 16].
  • Public Content: Content posted to Feeds or Reels is public and intended for distribution[cite: 17, 35].

B. Device Permissions

To function correctly, we request access to[cite: 19]:

  • Camera & Microphone: For capturing Reels and sending media[cite: 20].
  • Media Gallery: To select photos/videos for your profile and posts[cite: 21].
  • Contacts (Optional): To help you connect with people you know[cite: 22].

C. Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Logs: Device model, OS version, and performance reports for troubleshooting[cite: 24, 25].
  • IP Address: Briefly collected during login for security and access prevention[cite: 26].

3. Data Storage & Retention

Storage Infrastructure: All user data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by DigitalOcean[cite: 39].

  • Public Content: Retained while your account is active[cite: 40].
  • Account Data: Kept as long as the account exists[cite: 41].
  • Encrypted Messages: Stored only until successfully delivered; they are then purged from our infrastructure[cite: 42, 43].

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use limited trusted providers to maintain app stability[cite: 45]:

  • Infrastructure: DigitalOcean handles backend hosting[cite: 47].
  • Framework: Built using Expo and React Native; we may use Expo's internal tools for crash reporting[cite: 48, 49].

Strict Privacy: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or Meta-owned tracking SDKs[cite: 50, 51].

5. User Rights & Account Deletion

In compliance with Google Play requirements, we provide full control over your digital identity[cite: 53]:

  • In-App Deletion: Permanently delete your account via Settings > Account > Delete Account[cite: 55].
  • Web-Based Deletion: If the app is uninstalled, requests can be submitted at ireshp.com/delete-account[cite: 56].
  • Data Purge: Upon deletion, all public posts, reels, profile info, and undelivered messages are permanently removed from production databases[cite: 57].

6. Contact & Updates

We may update this policy to reflect service or legal changes and will notify you within the app[cite: 59, 60].
